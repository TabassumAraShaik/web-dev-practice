Spiced Chai Biscuit Delight

A warm and comforting tea snack inspired by rainy-day vibes and cozy anime kitchens, this recipe blends chai spices with buttery biscuits for a nostalgic treat.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk infused with chai spices
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven and line a baking tray.
  2. Mix the flour, sugar, and baking powder in a bowl.
  3. Stir in the chai-infused milk and melted butter to form a dough.
  4. Shape biscuits, place on the tray, and bake until golden.
